Shipping

All products are made to order, and we will contact you via email with an estimated delivery date once your order has been processed.

Please expect a minimum of three weeks for your order to be delivered. If your item is in stock, it will be delivered within 3-5 working days.

Returns

If you change your mind, we offer a 14-day return window from the date of delivery.

The jewelry must not have been worn and must be in its original case with the invoice and certificate of authenticity.

Personalized items cannot be exchanged. Please note that all items with custom engravings cannot be returned.
